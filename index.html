About the University

Uttarakhand University of Horticulture & Forestry

Shaping Green Futures in the Himalayan Region

Established in 2011 by the Government of Uttarakhand (Act No. 8 of 2011), UUHF is a premier institution dedicated to horticulture, forestry, and allied sciences. Headquartered at Bharsar, Pauri Garhwal, the university was carved out of G.B. Pant University of Agriculture & Technology to address the unique agro-climatic and ecological needs of Uttarakhand.

Vision
Global center of excellence in horticulture & forestry education.
Mission
Need-based research, technology transfer, biodiversity conservation.

Atmospheric Forecasting for Seasonal Advisory & Natural Analytics

AFSANA – Real-time Weather & Farm Solutions

Empowering farmers and researchers with location-specific weather, forecasting, disaster alerts, and historical analytics for Uttarakhand's complex terrain.

📍 Location-based Weather

GPS or manual location (Tehri, Ranichauri, Jagdhar) – real-time temp, humidity, wind, rainfall.

📆 7‑Day Forecast

Hourly forecasts, extreme weather alerts, and farming recommendations based on upcoming conditions.

🌾 Agricultural Advisory

Crop-specific sowing/harvesting windows, irrigation alerts, pest control & fertilizer guidance.

⚠️ Disaster & Alert System

Early warnings for heavy rain, landslides, storms, snowfall – push/SMS alerts for hilly regions.

📊 Historical Analytics

Past weather trends, temperature/rainfall analysis, graphs – available for research & agribusiness.

🌐 Integrated Dashboard

Access all modules via one interface – coming soon for farmers and extension officers.

Official Government Initiatives

Weather & Farmer Advisory Apps

Reliable tools by IMD, IITM, and Govt of India – free for all citizens and farmers.

Mausam

India Meteorological Department

Current weather, city-wise forecasts, temperature, humidity, wind, and weather warnings.

Meghdoot

IMD + ICAR

Weather-based agro-advisory, district-level crop guidance, sowing, irrigation & spraying recommendations.

Damini

IITM Pune

Real-time lightning detection & alerts within 20 km radius, safety guidelines for farmers.
